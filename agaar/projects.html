Projects We Specialize

These are some of the types of projects we have expertise in.

Solar Street Light Installations

The solar street light installation project is a good and convenient initiative to promote clean energy and reduce the use of fossil fuels. The county governments have embraced these projects during the last fiscal year and the implimentation is great.

Solar Water Heating Installations

A good rule of thumb is to choose a solar water heating installation project that will provide 80% savings to your current electric bill. Generally, that would require a 4kW system or larger, and in Agaar we will get all that done and get you up and running within a day.

Solar Irrigation Projects

Most new generation solar irrigators are very efficient and does not emit toxic vapour in the environment. In addition, it has a small environmental footprint in comparison to other sources of irrigation. The packages includes a solar water irrigation system, solar photovoltaic panels, and an energy storage system.

Solar PV Installation Projects

In areas that have a lot of hot days which make it difficult for the solar panels to work at peak capacity, it is important to install the right size of solar panel or there might be no savings at all. Agaar company has done a lot of research on this topic and can offer solutions in areas that have enough sun exposure for 8 hours a day.
